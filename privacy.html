Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-23

This Privacy Policy explains how Zero Office handles data for its internal creator workflow and TikTok integration.

1. Data We Collect

Zero Office stores only the minimum data required to operate the workflow, which may include OAuth token data, connected account identifiers, upload status, clip identifiers, timestamps, captions, basic video metadata, and analytics records needed by the creator dashboard.

2. How We Use Data

Data is used to connect the creator's own social media account, submit approved videos, track upload status, map platform post IDs to internal clip IDs, and show creator-side analytics.

3. TikTok Data

If the creator connects TikTok, Zero Office may use TikTok OAuth and approved TikTok API scopes to identify the connected account and publish approved videos. Zero Office does not scrape TikTok and does not sell TikTok user data.

4. Data Sharing

Zero Office does not sell personal data. Data may be sent to TikTok only when required to perform the creator-approved TikTok integration, such as authentication or video publishing.

5. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens and technical workflow data should be stored securely by the creator in local environment files or secure storage. Secrets must not be committed to public repositories.

6. Data Deletion

The creator can revoke TikTok access in TikTok settings and delete locally stored workflow data from the Zero Office project files.
